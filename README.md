The assessment is from module 2 . In this module studied about NumPy and Pandas series , data ingestion, data wrangling,  data exploration, data visualisation, web scraping and git.

In this project analyzes I'll be working with a dataset from ABC company, consisting of 458 rows and 9 columns. The company requires a comprehensive report detailing information about their employees across various teams. The objective of this analysis is to understand work distribution, salary expenditure, age grouping, and the relationship between age and salary. Through data preprocessing, statistical analysis, graphical visualization techniques such as Matplotlib and Seaborn were used to cover meaningful insights from the data set.

The height column was corrected by random numbers between 150 and 180. To ensure data consistency before proceeding with the analysis. 

In the employee distribution across teams is uneven. some teams have higher number of employees compared to others.
Most employees fall within the mid-age category (25-30) , indicating that the organization primarily consists of experienced professionals. This suggests a balanced work with both stability and productivity.
Salary expenditure varies significantly across teams and positions. Certain teams contribute more heavily to overall payroll expenses, possibly due to higher staffing levels or higher-paying job roles.

Through data cleaning, aggregation, visualization, and correlation analysis, raw data was transformed into meaningful business insights that can assist management in workforce planning and strategic decision-making.

All visualizations were created using Matplotlib and Seaborn to ensure clear and professional representation of findings.
